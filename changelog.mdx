## Upgrade Process

Follow these steps to upgrade your SlicedHealth instance safely.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Backup Data" icon="database">
    Export your current configurations and datasets.

    ```
    curl -H "Authorization: Bearer YOUR_TOKEN" \
         https://api.example.com/v1/export \
         -o backup.json
    ```
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review Changes" icon="book-open">
    Check the breaking changes section above and update your integrations.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Deploy Update" icon="rocket">
    Use your deployment tool to apply the new version.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify" icon="check-circle">
    Test key features like SlicedIQ predictions and variance analysis.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip" title="Pro Tip">
Test upgrades in a staging environment first to avoid disruptions to production revenue workflows.
</Callout>
