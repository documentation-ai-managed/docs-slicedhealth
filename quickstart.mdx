## Prerequisites

<Callout kind="info" title="Before You Begin">
Ensure you have:
- A valid email address for registration
- Access to your organization's billing and claims data
- Browser compatibility: Chrome, Firefox, or Safari (latest versions)
</Callout>

Review your healthcare revenue management needs, such as contract variance analysis or price transparency compliance.

## Create Your Account

Follow these steps to register for SlicedHealth.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Registration Page" icon="user-plus">
    Navigate to the SlicedHealth sign-up page at `https://slicedhealth.com/register`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Your Details" icon="edit-3">
    Provide your organization name, email, and role. Agree to the terms of service.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox for a verification email and click the link to activate your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Password" icon="lock">
    Create a strong password meeting the security requirements.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Access the Portals

SlicedHealth provides separate portals for clients and billing.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Client Login" icon="user">
    Use the client portal to access SlicedIQ, variance analysis, and business intelligence tools.

    1. Go to `https://app.slicedhealth.com/client/login`
    2. Enter your email and password
    3. Enable two-factor authentication if prompted
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Billing Login" icon="dollar-sign">
    Manage invoices and payments through the billing portal.

    1. Visit `https://billing.slicedhealth.com/login`
    2. Use your billing credentials
    3. Review your subscription status
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Callout kind="tip">
Save your login URLs in your browser for quick access.
</Callout>

## Connect Your First Data Source

Upload claims data or connect via API to start analysis.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Data Sources" icon="database">
    From the dashboard, select **Data Connections** in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Upload Method" icon="upload">
    Select **File Upload** for CSV/Excel or **API Integration** for live data.
  </Step>
  <Step title="API Connection Example" icon="link">
    <CodeGroup tabs="cURL,JavaScript">
````bash
curl -X POST https://api.example.com/v1/data/connect \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer YOUR_API_KEY" \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
  -d '{
    "source": "claims_db",
    "endpoint": "postgres://user:pass@localhost:5432/claims"
  }'
````
````javascript
const response = await fetch('https://api.example.com/v1/data/connect', {
  method: 'POST',
  headers: {
    'Authorization': 'Bearer YOUR_API_KEY',
    'Content-Type': 'application/json',
  },
  body: JSON.stringify({
    source: 'claims_db',
    endpoint: 'postgres://user:pass@localhost:5432/claims'
  })
});
````
    </CodeGroup>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Connection" icon="check-circle">
    Monitor the status in **Data Sources**. Expect initial processing within minutes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Expandable title="Troubleshooting Common Issues" default-open="false">
- **Login fails?** Reset password via `https://app.slicedhealth.com/forgot-password`.
- **Data upload errors?** Ensure files are UTF-8 encoded and under 100MB.
- **API 401 error?** Regenerate your `{API_KEY}` in account settings.
</Expandable>
