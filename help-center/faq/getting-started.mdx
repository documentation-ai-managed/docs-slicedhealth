## Getting Started

1. Sign up at `https://dashboard.slicedhealth.com`.
2. Verify your email address.
3. Log in to access your workspace.

## Create Your First Project

1. Click "New Project"
2. Enter a project name and description
3. Click "Create"

You can now organize your documentation in your new project.
