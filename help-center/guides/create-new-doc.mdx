## Quick Guide

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your project">
    Go to your Slicedhealth workspace and select your project.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a new page">
    Click the "Add Page" button in the project menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure the page">
    Enter a title and optional description for your new page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Write your content">
    Use the editor to add documentation. Format with headings, lists, and code snippets as needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save changes">
    Click "Save" to publish your new documentation page instantly.
  </Step>
</Steps>
