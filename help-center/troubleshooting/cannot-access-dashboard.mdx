description: How to fix dashboard login issues.
---

## Problem

- Unable to log in to Slicedhealth dashboard
- Error message: "Authentication failed"

## Solutions

<Steps>
  <Step title="Reset your password">
    ````
    Use the "Forgot password" link on the login page to create a new password.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear browser cache">
    ````
    Clear cookies and cache, then reload the page.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check email verification">
    ````
    Ensure your email is verified; check your inbox or spam folder.
    ````
  </Step>
</Steps>
