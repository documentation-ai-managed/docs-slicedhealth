## Overview

SlicedHealth empowers healthcare providers with advanced revenue management tools. You uncover hidden underpayments, analyze contract variances in real-time, and ensure compliance through AI-driven insights. Key solutions include SlicedIQ for intelligent analysis, variance tracking, business intelligence dashboards, price transparency reporting, and claim estimation for upfront payments.

Targeted at hospitals and specialty practices, SlicedHealth helps you capture every earned dollar by providing actionable data and automation.

## Quick Start

Get up and running with SlicedHealth in minutes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Account" icon="user-plus">
    Visit the client login portal and sign up with your organization details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox for verification and activate your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access Dashboard" icon="layout-dashboard">
    Log in at `https://dashboard.slicedhealth.com` and explore your revenue insights.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect Data" icon="database">
    Upload your first claims data or connect via API for real-time analysis.
  </Step>
</Steps>
